"Nobody asked me to save these people.
That's how I know it's important."

  • The Rapture arrives in 30 seconds. Father Flanagan knows this because he did the math on a napkin.
  • Below his gold tower: ordinary people. Walking around. Existing. Legally.
  • His plan — and now, regrettably, yours — is to throw Bibles at strangers, from a tower, until they are "saved."
  • A direct hit converts one (1) soul. The soul's opinion on this was not collected.
